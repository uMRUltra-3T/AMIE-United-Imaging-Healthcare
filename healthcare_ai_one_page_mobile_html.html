Section B

Herbal Medicine & Traditional Medicine (TCM) AI

Model Choices

General LLMs (GPT-4o / Qwen 2.5 Max / Doubao 1.5 Pro) show the strongest performance on real-case TCM tasks to date.

Domain models (TCM-GPT, HuatuoGPT, ShenNong-TCM) suit custom tuning, protocols, and institutional knowledge.

Safety Stack

Combine an LLM + RAG over pharmacopeias + a herb–drug interaction knowledge graph to surface contraindications, ADR warnings, and dosage checks with citations.

Typical Flow
  1. Intake & symptoms ⇢ TCM pattern identification
  2. Candidate formula suggestion
  3. Herb–drug interaction & contraindication check
  4. Clinician review & finalization
